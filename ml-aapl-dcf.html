Methodology

1

Historical Data Collection

Extracted 5-year financials from Apple's 10-K SEC filings (FY2019–FY2024) — Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement.

2

3-Statement Model Build

Built fully integrated 3-statement model in Excel with dynamic drivers — revenue growth, margin assumptions, working capital, and CapEx schedules.

3

DCF Valuation

Projected 5-year free cash flows, calculated terminal value using Gordon Growth Model, and discounted at WACC to derive enterprise and equity value.

4

Sensitivity Analysis

Built a two-variable sensitivity table across WACC (7%–12%) and terminal growth rate (2%–4%) to stress-test the base case valuation.

5

Segment & Peer Analysis

Disaggregated revenue and margin by segment; benchmarked across MSFT and GOOGL on 8 financial KPIs to triangulate valuation.

6

Investment Recommendation

Synthesized DCF output, peer comps, and segment thesis into a BUY recommendation presented to faculty investment committee at Goldey-Beacom College.
