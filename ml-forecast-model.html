Rolling Lookup
=INDEX(Revenue,MATCH(1,(Month=A2)*(Year=B2),0))
Pulls actual revenue for each period dynamically as the model rolls forward month by month.
Variance %
=IFERROR((Actual-Budget)/ABS(Budget),0)
Calculates percentage variance safely — handles zero-budget denominator without errors.
Forecast Lock
=IF(MonthNum<=CurrentMonth,Actual,Forecast)
Auto-switches between actuals and forecast depending on current reporting period.
Dept Rollup
=SUMIFS(Amount,Dept,D2,Period,">="&Start,Period,"<="&End)
Aggregates costs by department and date range for variance reporting by business unit.
Seasonal Adjustment
=BaseRev*XLOOKUP(MonthNum,SeasonTbl[Month],SeasonTbl[Idx])
Applies seasonal index multipliers to smooth revenue forecasts for cyclical demand patterns.
DAX — Rolling 3M Avg
CALCULATE(AVERAGE([Revenue]),DATESINPERIOD('Date'[Date],LASTDATE('Date'[Date]),-3,MONTH))
Power BI DAX for trailing 3-month average KPI used on executive dashboard.
Dynamic Array Filter
=FILTER(ModelData,(ModelData[BU]=SelBU)*(ModelData[Month]=SelMo))
Dynamically filters model output by business unit and month for slice-and-dice analysis.
Waterfall Component
=IF(C4>=0,C4,0) / =IF(C4<0,ABS(C4),0)
Splits variance into positive and negative components to drive the waterfall bridge chart.
Goal Seek Target
Tools → What-If → Goal Seek → Set GM% = 35% by changing COGS%
Used to back-solve required COGS reduction to hit target gross margin for scenario planning.
